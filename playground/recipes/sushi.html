Sushi

Description

Sushi is a timeless classic - easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Eggs
  • Ham
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Place rice on seaweed evenly
  2. Chop all other ingredients
  3. Place ingredients evenly on top of rice
  4. Roll seaweed into cylinder
  5. Cut the cylinder into even pieces
